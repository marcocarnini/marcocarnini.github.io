About me

I am a Lead Data Scientist at Features Analytics, a Belgian Fintech company. I build Machine Learning models and look for anomalies in large datasets.

Working Experience

  • February 2017 – Present: Features Analytics, working with R and Python for rapid prototyping, Machine Learning and Data Analysis.

  • November 2011 – January 2017: Johannes Gutentberg Universität, working with R, Python and C++ in Bioinformatics. Also, working as a Tutor, Teaching Assistant and Lecturer.

  • June 2006 – October 2011: Politecnico di Milano, working as a Tutor and Teaching Assistant for Theoretical Mechanics, Calculus, Linear Algebra, MATLAB, R and Introductory Data Analysis.

Education

  • November 2011 – January 2017: Post Doc in Bioinfomatics at Johannes Gutentberg Universität, studying proteins and Machine Learning.

  • January 2006 – March 2010: Ph.D. in Mathematical Engineering at Politecnico di Milano, studying Rarefied Gas Dynamics and Numerical Methods.

  • September 2000 – October 2005: Master of Science Università degli studi di Milano, studying Theoretical Physics and Statistical Mechanics.
